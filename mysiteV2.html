About Me

A Story of Innovation and Impact

From my first line of code to developing complex AI models, my journey in technology has been driven by a single question: "How can this make a difference?" As an Electronics and Communication Engineering student, I discovered my passion lies at the intersection of embedded systems and artificial intelligence—a space where I can create tangible solutions for real-world humanitarian challenges.

This led me to develop an AI-powered communication system for children with Cerebral Palsy and Autism, a project that cemented my commitment to assistive technology. Whether it's winning hackathons like the Smart India Hackathon or filing patents for safety devices, my goal remains the same: to leverage technology to empower communities and protect lives. I am constantly learning, building, and seeking new ways to turn innovative ideas into impactful realities.
